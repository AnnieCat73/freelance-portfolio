Why I made this project

I wanted to take on the challenge of making my first HTML template and making it available initially for free for anybody who might need such a template for a project. I thought a template for a SAAS product would be more interesting but decided to go with accountancy as I know of a potential client who may need one in the future.

Research

Process

I looked at all the various platforms selling templates and found that there weren’t that many for accountancy. They aren’t probably the most popular but the few good ones I saw had really good sales numbers. Business templates in general are among the most popular on these market places.

Idea

I had the idea of having an accountancy firm helping small businesses with their accounting needs. Many small firms struggle with keeping up with their paperwork which may lead to stressful situations. It’s similar to what I do but this is taken from an accounting perspective.

Persona

I created a persona based on a potential client of this accountancy firm, the small business owner.

Goals

  • Help to easily file taxes
  • Alleviate stress by keeping books clean
  • Less stress and predictable finances lead to more time for growing their business

Frustrations

  • Too busy to keep up!
  • Overwhelmed by unending paperwork
  • May be paying too much or worried about penalties if paying too little tax!

About Samantha

Age: 32

Business: Restaurant owner

Location: London

Meet Samantha, a 32-year-old restaurant owner based in London. Sarah has always had a passion for food and decided to turn her passion into a career by opening her own restaurant. However, the day-to-day running of the business has left her with little time to focus on her accounting needs.

Sarah is struggling to keep up with her accounting responsibilities despite her busy schedule running her restaurant. She wants to streamline her accounting process and free up more time to focus on growing her business.
