How I work

My Process

01

Phase 1

Discovery Call

I will look into your world to gain a thorough understanding of you, your pain points, and your target audience. This is important for designing a high-performing website that meets your needs.

02

Phase 2

Design Phase

I will use what I have learned about you to create a custom website that meets your unique needs, accurately reflects your brand, and is visually appealing and user-friendly for your audience.

03

Phase 3

Build Phase

Once you approve the designs, I will proceed to buildig them ensuring optimal adherence to modern web-standards. I aim to deliver a high-performance, secure, and reliable solution for your needs.

04

Phase 4

Launch Phase

Upon completing the build and receiving your approval, it's time to launch your website. In this phase, I will offer you training so you will be able to add content to your website on your own.

Who is Annie

About Me

I am Annie and I am a freelance web designer and developer who lives in London, with my daughter and 3 cats!

Before I entered the world of tech I did a variety of roles, some of which include running my own business and gaining a degree in Politics and Development Studies.

I enjoy crafting beautiful and user-friendly websites and I am always looking to expand my knowledge and grow my skills as a designer and developer.

I challenge myself to think outside the box! If I don’t know something, I learn it. If I see a problem, I find a solution.
