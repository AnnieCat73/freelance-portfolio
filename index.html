How I work

My Process

01

Phase 1

Discovery Call

I will delve deep into your world to fully comprehend who you are, what your problems are, and who your target market is. This is crucial for creating a website that performs well and serves your goals.

02

Phase 2

Designing your website

Using what I have discovered about you, I will develop a custom website that fulfils your specific requirements, accurately represents your brand, and is both aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly for your target audience.

03

Phase 3

Building your website

Once you provide your approval, I will start building them while ensuring that they adhere as closely as possible to current web standards. I want to meet your needs with a high-performing, secure, and dependable solution.

04

Phase 4

Launching your website

After the build is finished and you have approved of the website, the time has come to launch your website. I will provide you with training during this phase so you can update your website with new content on your own.

Who is Annie

About Me

I am Annie and I am a freelance web designer and developer who lives in London, with my daughter and 3 cats!

Before I entered the world of tech I did a variety of roles, some of which include running my own business and gaining a degree in Politics and Development Studies.

I enjoy crafting beautiful and user-friendly websites and I am always looking to expand my knowledge and grow my skills as a designer and developer.

I like a challenge! If there is something I don’t know, I learn it. If I have a problem, I'll figure out a solution!
